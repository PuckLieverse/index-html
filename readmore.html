About

Expand here



During Summer term 2024 at University College Utrecht, I followed the Lab Course Geographical Information Systems (GIS) at the Copernicus Institute of Sustainable Development, taught by Dr. Britta Ricker.
Here, I learned how to process spatial data, execute spatial analysis for decision making and apply the principles of carthography to real-life scenarios.

The projects I worked on are related to landscape management and sustainability, specifically in the context of the conservation of nature in Utrecht.
